What is QuBE?

QuBE is an application that measures myocardial perfusion on coronary angiograms in patients who have undergone percutaneous angioplasty for acute myocardial infarction. More information is available in the Publication about QuBE (Vogelzang, M., Vlaar, P. J., Svilaas, T., Amo, D., Nijsten, M. W.N., Zijlstra, F. (2009). Computer-assisted myocardial blush quantification after percutaneous coronary angioplasty for acute myocardial infarction: a substudy from the TAPAS trial. Eur Heart J 30: 594-599).
